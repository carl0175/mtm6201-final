Our Story

The Green Door began as a small, community-driven initiative in Ottawa with a simple mission: to serve wholesome, plant-based meals rooted in sustainability, ethics, and care. Founded by a group of passionate food lovers and environmental advocates, the restaurant opened its doors with a commitment to sourcing local, organic ingredients and preparing meals that nourish both body and planet.

Over the years, The Green Door grew into a beloved gathering place for those seeking conscious dining—welcoming yoga practitioners, students, families, and eco-conscious eaters alike. Its name symbolizes both a literal entry into a green, sustainable space and a metaphorical gateway to mindful living.

With a focus on transparency, comfort, and culinary excellence, The Green Door continues to evolve while staying true to its roots: delivering food that’s good for people and the planet, without compromising on flavor or integrity.

Our Values

1. Sustainability First

We are committed to protecting the planet through environmentally responsible practices — from local sourcing to zero-waste efforts — every decision begins with sustainability.

2. Ethical Sourcing

Our ingredients come from trusted local farms and ethical producers. We believe in full transparency, fair partnerships, and conscious consumption.

3. Plant-Based Nourishment

We craft meals that nourish the body and mind using only wholesome, plant-based ingredients — rooted in nature, full of flavor, and good for all.

4. Comfort & Care

We create spaces, dishes, and experiences designed to make guests feel welcome, relaxed, and respected — because food should feel as good as it tastes.

5. Community Connection

We actively support and collaborate with Ottawa’s wellness, arts, and local food communities. The Green Door is a space for gathering, sharing, and growing together.

6. Mindful Efficiency

We value your time and experience. From ordering to dining, we aim to deliver thoughtful, streamlined service — always with intention and integrity.

Mission Statement

At The Green Door, our mission is to serve thoughtfully prepared, plant-based meals that nourish the body, respect the planet, and support our local community. We are committed to sustainability, ethical sourcing, and mindful hospitality — creating a space where comfort, care, and conscious living come together with every plate.
