Menu

1. Coconut Chickpea Curry

A creamy, mildly spiced curry made with chickpeas, coconut milk, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and turmeric. Served over jasmine rice with fresh cilantro and lime.

2. Smoky BBQ Jackfruit Wrap

Pulled jack fruit in a tangy BBQ sauce, served in a warm tortilla with slaw, avocado, and chipolata ravioli.

3. Creamy Cashew Alfredo Pasta

Linguine tossed in a rich, velvety cashew cream sauce with garlic sautéed mushrooms, spinach, and cherry tomatoes.

4. Mediterranean Power Bowl

Quinoa, roasted chickpeas, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, kalamata olives, and tahini-lemon dressing. Garnished with fresh parsley and sumac.

5. Black Bean & Sweet Potato Burger

House-made patty topped with avocado, lettuce, tomato, pickled onions, and vegan mayo on a whole grain bun. Served with baked sweet potato wedges.

6. Tofu Banh Mi Sandwich

Crispy tofu, pickled carrots and daikon, cucumber, jalapeños, cilantro, and vegan sriracha mayo on a toasted baguette.

7. Miso Ramen with Crispy Tofu

Umami-rich miso broth with noodles, bok choy, mushrooms, corn, green onions, and sesame-crusted tofu.

8. Cauliflower Tinga Tacos

Soft corn tortillas filled with roasted cauliflower in a spicy tomato-chipotle sauce. Topped with avocado crema, red cabbage slaw, and lime.

Our Sources: The Journey from Farm to Plate

We believe in food transparency. Our sourcing information is collected from reputable agricultural and environmental resources to ensure accuracy and accountability: Harvest Dates: Local crop data provided by Foodland Ontario and Ontario Ministry of Agriculture. Distance Traveled: Calculated from farm to restaurant using route planning tools like Google Maps. Seasonal Availability: Cross-referenced with regional growing calendars and supplier harvest schedules. Our mission is to shorten the distance between you and your food — and show you every step of the way.
